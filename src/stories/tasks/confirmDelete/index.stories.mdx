# ConfirmDelete

**Confirms deletion or refreshing of an org with unretrieved changes.**

## Overview

The **ConfirmDelete** component is an
[SLDS Modal](http://design-system-react-components.herokuapp.com/?path=/story/sldsmodal--modal-with-custom-parent-node)
used to confirm the deletion of a **Scratch**, **Dev**, or
**Test Org** with unretrieved changes.

For `Project` **Scratch Orgs**, the modal is also triggered when attempting to refresh an org with unretrieved changes.
